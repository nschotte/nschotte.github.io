Our Story

Welcome to Osteria Schottenfeld, where the heart of Italy meets the charm of Mill Valley. Founded by Nick Schottenfeld, our restaurant is a tribute to the time-honored traditions of Italian cooking, crafted with passion and served with warmth. Inspired by the rustic osterias of Italy—where friends and family gather over simple yet exquisite meals—our kitchen brings you authentic flavors, fresh ingredients, and a dining experience that feels like home.

The story of Osteria Schottenfeld begins with a love for Italian cuisine passed down through generations. Nick Schottenfeld, a dedicated chef and food enthusiast, spent years traveling through Italy, learning the secrets of traditional pasta-making, wood-fired cooking, and regional specialties from the kitchens of local artisans. With a deep appreciation for the slow food philosophy and high-quality ingredients, he envisioned a space in Mill Valley where the beauty of Italian dining could be celebrated.

Our menu is a reflection of this journey—featuring handmade pastas, fresh seafood, and rustic meats, all prepared with the finest locally sourced and imported ingredients. From the rich, truffle-infused Tagliatelle al Tartufo Nero to the delicate, herb-crusted Branzino al Limone, every dish is crafted to transport you to the vibrant markets of Florence, the coastal towns of Amalfi, or the rolling vineyards of Tuscany. Complemented by an expertly curated selection of Italian wines and house-crafted cocktails, our offerings ensure that every meal is an unforgettable experience.

At Osteria Schottenfeld, we believe that dining is about more than just food—it’s about connection, storytelling, and tradition. Whether you're here for a romantic dinner, a lively family gathering, or simply a glass of wine at our cozy bar, we welcome you into our kitchen with open arms. Step inside, savor the flavors, and let us take you on a journey through Italy, one plate at a time.

Benvenuti a casa—welcome home.
