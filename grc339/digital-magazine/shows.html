Shows & Setlists — Best of the Best

A curated snapshot of era-defining performances by the Grateful Dead and Dead & Company. Click any date to expand the full setlist. These are canon picks—widely cited by tapers, archivists, and fans.

Grateful Dead — 1970s

BEST OF THE BEST May 8, 1977 — Ithaca, NY — Barton Hall (Cornell)
Often ranked their greatest show — pristine playing & recording.
  1. Set I: New Minglewood Blues · Loser · El Paso · They Love Each Other · Jack Straw · Deal · Lazy Lightnin’ > Supplication · Brown Eyed Women · Mama Tried · Row Jimmy · Dancing in the Street
  2. Set II: Scarlet Begonias > Fire on the Mountain · Estimated Prophet · St. Stephen · Not Fade Away · St. Stephen · Morning Dew
  3. Encore: One More Saturday Night

Grateful Dead — 1960s

Feb 27, 1969 — Fillmore West, San Francisco
Classic “Dark Star → St. Stephen → The Eleven” sequence immortalized on Live/Dead.
  1. Morning Dew · School Girl · Doin’ That Rag · King Bee · Cosmic Charlie · Lovelight · Dupree’s Diamond Blues · Mountains of the Moon · Dark Star · St. Stephen · The Eleven · Death Don’t Have No Mercy · Other One Suite · Alligator · Drums · Jam · Caution · Feedback · We Bid You Goodnight

Grateful Dead — 1980s

Jul 24, 1987 — Oakland, CA — Dylan & The Dead
GD sets + full Bob Dylan set backed by the Dead.
  1. GD Set I: Jack Straw · Half-Step Uptown · Cassidy · Deal …
  2. GD Set II: Scarlet Begonias · Playing in the Band · UJB · Dear Mr. Fantasy · Sugar Magnolia …
  3. Dylan: Times They Are A-Changin’ · Maggie’s Farm · Watchtower …

Dead & Company — 2010s

Nov 17, 2017 — Boston, MA — TD Garden
Modern standout with “Milestones” and a hushed “Ripple.”

  2. Set II: Scarlet Begonias > Fire · He’s Gone · Viola Lee Blues · Milestones · Wharf Rat · Sugar Magnolia
  3. Encore: Ripple
