Introduction

The Long Strange Trip (Deadhead Culture)

The Long Strange Trip (Deadhead Culture) is a digital magazine celebrating the creativity and spirit of Deadheads, the devoted community around the Grateful Dead since the 1960s. From Shakedown Street’s marketplace to Spinners’ dancing and Wharf Rats’ sobriety, Deadhead culture is diverse and ever-evolving. Today it thrives through music, art, fashion, and community, uniting generations more than half a century later.
